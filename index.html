About the Artist

Bhajan Gayan of Vrindavan

Dhruv Ladla Ji is a revered bhajan singer rooted in the sacred soil of Shri Vrindavan Dham — the eternal land of Radha and Krishna. With a divine voice that carries the fragrance of Brij Bhoomi, he has been captivating devotees across India with his soul-stirring Bhajan Sandhyas and melodious kirtans for over 15 years.

"यह चैनल श्री वृन्दावन धाम के रसास्वादन हेतु मधुर मधुर भजनों से सज्जित है — भजन गायक श्री ध्रुव लाडला जी के भजन संध्याओं की अनुपम प्रस्तुतियों का संग्रह।"

His compositions draw from the timeless bhajan traditions of saints like Kunj Bihari Das Ji Maharaj, fusing classical ragas with the pure devotion of Vraja. Every performance becomes a Bhakti Yatra — a spiritual journey that transforms hearts. His YouTube channel regularly hosts live Bhajan Sandhyas that bring the divine atmosphere of Vrindavan to devotees worldwide.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book Dhruv Ladla Ji for a program?
We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance for local programs, and 4–8 weeks for programs requiring travel. During peak festival seasons like Janmashtami, Navratri, and Diwali, we advise booking 2–3 months in advance as dates fill up quickly. However, we always try our best to accommodate last-minute requests — please contact us via WhatsApp (+91 7248252881) or email (dhruv7248ladla@gmail.com) for emergency bookings.
What is the travel coverage area for programs?
Dhruv Ladla Ji is available across all Indian states. We regularly perform in Delhi NCR, Uttar Pradesh (Mathura, Agra, Lucknow, Varanasi), Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, Maharashtra, and other states. For international NRI community programs (USA, UK, Canada, Australia), special arrangements can be made — please contact us well in advance for international bookings.
What is included in the professional sound system?
For full Bhajan Sandhya programs, we provide professional PA sound system, microphones, monitor speakers, and all cables/equipment. For smaller home programs, we bring portable sound systems appropriate for the venue size. You do not need to arrange any audio equipment separately. For outdoor programs with audiences of 500+, we recommend dedicated PA rental which we help coordinate.
Can I request specific bhajans or a custom setlist?
Absolutely! We highly encourage devotees to share their favorite bhajans and specific requests. Dhruv Ladla Ji has an extensive repertoire of hundreds of bhajans spanning various saints and traditions — Mirabai, Surdas, Tulsidas, Kabir Das, and the Vrindavan tradition. Simply share your preferences during the booking consultation and we will craft a personalized setlist for your occasion.
How can I contact Dhruv Ladla Ji directly?
You can reach us through multiple channels: WhatsApp (preferred) at +91 7248252881 — this is the fastest way to get a response. Email us at dhruv7248ladla@gmail.com for detailed enquiries, quotes, and booking confirmations. You can also call directly at +91 7248252881. For general programme updates and content, follow our YouTube channel @dhruvladla6062 and Instagram @dhruvladlaji.
Is live YouTube streaming included in programs?
Yes! Live YouTube streaming can be arranged for programs as an add-on service. This is especially popular for temple mahotsavs, large Bhajan Sandhyas, and family events where relatives from other cities or abroad wish to participate virtually. We handle the complete streaming setup — camera, internet connectivity requirements, and YouTube channel coordination. The streamed recording is also available to keep afterwards.
What is the pricing structure for programs?
Pricing varies based on event type, duration, location, number of artists required, and any special arrangements. Home Puja programs start from a different range than full Bhajan Sandhya programs with a complete band. We believe in transparent, honest pricing — please reach out via WhatsApp or email with your event details and we will provide a custom quote promptly. There are no hidden charges.
Are programs available for NRI communities abroad?
Yes! Dhruv Ladla Ji serves Indian/Hindu diaspora communities in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and other countries. For physical presence abroad, please plan at least 3–4 months in advance for visa and travel arrangements. Alternatively, we conduct highly popular online live Bhajan Sandhyas via YouTube where NRI families across the world can participate in real-time. Contact us at dhruv7248ladla@gmail.com for international booking enquiries.
