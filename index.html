About the Artist

Bhajan Gayan of Vrindavan

Dhruv Ladla Ji is a revered bhajan singer rooted in the sacred soil of Shri Vrindavan Dham — the eternal land of Radha and Krishna. With a divine voice that carries the fragrance of Brij Bhoomi, he has been captivating devotees across India with his melodious Bhajan Sandhyas and soul-stirring kirtans.

"यह चैनल श्री वृन्दावन धाम के रसास्वादन हेतु मधुर मधुर भजनों से सज्जित है — भजन गायक श्री ध्रुव लाडला जी के भजन संध्याओं की अनुपम प्रस्तुतियों का संग्रह।"

His compositions draw from the timeless bhajan traditions of saints like Kunj Bihari Das Ji Maharaj, fusing classical ragas with the pure devotion of Vraja. Every performance becomes a Bhakti Yatra — a spiritual journey inward.

Bhakti Programs & Services

From intimate family prayers to grand temple festivals — we bring divine melodies to every sacred occasion.

Bhajan Sandhya

A dedicated evening of devotional singing — immerse in the bliss of Radha-Krishna bhajans, specially curated for all spiritual gatherings, satsangs, and temple events.

Kirtan & Sankirtan

High-energy group kirtan sessions featuring traditional mridanga, harmonium, and cymbals. Perfect for Janmashtami, Ram Navami, Holi, and other religious festivals.

Griha Pravesh Bhajan

Bless your new home with sacred bhajans and Vedic hymns. A divine musical welcome that fills your abode with positive energy and divine blessings.

Temple Programs

Official Bhajan Sandhyas at temples across India. Dhruv Ladla Ji has performed at major temples in Vrindavan, Mathura, Haridwar, and beyond.

Festival Celebrations

Complete religious event management for Janmashtami, Radhashtami, Ram Navami, Holi and other sacred festivals with full band and sound arrangements.

Online Satsang & Live

Join the divine experience online! Live YouTube Bhajan Sandhyas bring Vrindavan to your home, wherever you are in the world. Pure bhakti at the click of a button.
